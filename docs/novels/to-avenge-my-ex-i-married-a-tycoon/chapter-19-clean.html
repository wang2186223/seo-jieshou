Chapter 19

Nathan walked out of the bushes only after making sure that the voices of Mr. and Mrs. Adams was far away. Shaking his head,he looked at Hilda who was clutching onto him like a koala.

Carrying Hilda, Nathan walked out of the hotel through the back and called Stanley to meet them at the back parking lot.

Arriving, Stanley was shocked when he saw Hilda in his bosses arms. He hurriedly opened the back door forhis boss. As he started the ignition and moved out of the parking lot, he asked "S-Sir, what is wrong with Madam?"

Nathan's eyes darkened. As Nathan's cold gaze met his, Stanley immediately let out a silly chuckle. "Where are we headed now Sir?"

Just as Nathan was about to speak to Nathan, he saw two Land Rovers coming on to them through the rear view mirror.

Nathan immediately asked Stanley to drive to his residence after which he called Samuel. "Samuel, I'm on the Lagos Avenue road now and there seems to be a few cars tailing after me. One of the cars number plate is xxxxxxxx. Help me find out who they are."

Hearing Nathan's serious tone, Samuel knew how serious the matter was. "Don't hang up yet. Try dodging them using the

Maseru street route and exciting the Lome street. But checking now, the car number plate is fake. How many cars are tailing you now?"

"A maximum of four cars. I thought it was Mr. Adams who asked them to tail Miss Adams now as she is with me but is seems otherwise." Nathan replied.

Samuel frowned before adding. "Hacking into their cellphones.they ain't speaking the local language but a foreign one. Which big shot did youa offend this time? Anyways I will intersect you guys on the Banana Street."

Samuel immediately drove out of his residence. Stepping on the accelerator he speed off like a gust of wind.

At that moment Hilda was still rubbing her body on Nathan.The drug has taken effect that she couldn't control herself anymore. Nothing made sense to her anymore. She tried removing the buttons of her top but Nathan beat her to it.Closing her buttons he nibbled on her ears. "Pumpkin, don't worry. It would all be over soon."

Hilda felt really awful as her body felt like it was been burnt on fire. She grabbed Nathan's hand and leaned over, making her lips naturally land on his. She kissed him eagerly without any restraint.

Nathan pulled her away, but she still clung onto him again.

The process repeated itself severally as the man could do nothing to hold her down anymore. Happily. Hilda brushed herself on his body.

Looking at the cars which were still speeding trying to overtake them. Nathan was beyond frustrated. Taking a deep breath.he held unto Hilda's hand before saying."Stop it."

Hearing him. Hilda frowned. She pouted pitifully and gentle nudged Nathan's chest with her head. "You are refusing me.You are bad!"

Nathan didn't know whether to laugh or cry when he heard Hilda. But looking back at the cars his gaze turned cold.

At the same time, Samuel who was coming to their rescue had already notified Kyle.

Kyle, Samuel, Nathan and Christopher were together known as the Big four in Taadi. Everyone of them was known for their ruthlessness. As Nathan's whose territory was the Business World,Samuel was into the Health Sector, Kyle was the Underworld and Christopher was an academy Best actor.This four have control of X country.

Since Kyle had control of the underworld, he already sent some forces to intersect the cars that was tailing Nathan. As soon as they all got to the Intersection, They covered Nathan's car as its speed off to another direction from home.

According to Samuel. after hacking into the phones of those tailing Nathan's car, he heard that there was already another group ready to ambush Nathan around his residence in case they failed to get him. So Nathan had to hear to another one of his villa's. As a business man who was into construction, he had soo many apartments and villa's another his name which he barely used.

Kyle was driving a Mercedes Benz where as Samuel was in a Bimmer. Kyle had just arrived at the crossroad and was about to ask Samuel who was still on the line with him to cross over to the other lane so he can handle the first two cars when his ears twitched and his expression changed drastically.

He immediately slammed on the steering wheel, as the whole Mercedes Benz made a creaking sound. Almost instantly, the car had barreled into the next lane hitting one of the Land rovers.

Immediately, the car summersaulted repeatedly before ending up in a pit and then catching fire. The car had noticed Nathan's car switching lane so it immediately wanted to catch up to it but unfortunately for him, Kyle noticed his plan and took him out.

Meanwhile, Samuel voice can be heard in Kyle's car as she spat."Wooh, that was a killer move there... Hahahaha."

Indifferently Kyle spoke up. "You should be taking this serious

you know!" Samuel had always been the silly type who acted as nothing but a silly husky.

"Whim! Whim!"

Samuel's Bimmer made a beast like roar as he speed past Kyle's for the other cars. Coming to an abrupt stop. it scratched in a long skid mark as it hit another Land rover which also summersaulted before catching fire. Making it two cars down.

At this time, Nathan has already arrived at another residence which was also his. As someone who was into real estate,he had apartments and building all over the city. Arriving, he rushed Hilda into the bedroom as she was still making moves on him.

Putting her onto the bed, Hilda cling onto him once again and kissed the corners of his lips. This time, her hands were eager to join the fun as they moved all over Nathan's body. She held his shirt and impatiently rubbed her body against him.

Nathan's gaze darkened as he gritted his teeth. "You started this Hilda."

He clasped the back of her head with his hand before he deepened the kiss. His other hand slide down her back slowly.His hands felt eager but trembled slightly. Hilda as completely given in and welcomed his actions but Nathan knew it was because of the drug.

With his last bit of rationality, he pulled back from the kiss.

Hilda looked at him pitifully with tears in her eyes which was going to fall at any moment. Looking at her like this. Nathan's heart ached.

He had never been a man of principle, but when it came to Hildda he didn't mind been patient. He also wanted to have her too but he was scared that she might wake up later after the drug has worn off and she might regret.

Pouting Hilda asked. "You don't want me?"

Shaking his head Nathan hurriedly explained. "No not that Pumpkin. I wouldn't want you to regret later."

Hilda held onto Nathan's hand and spoke with tears falling from her eyes. "I won't regret anything. I feel really terrible."

Gritting his teeth, Nathan asked. "Hilda, who am I?"

Looking at Nathan, Hilda frowned. Who is he? What question is that? "Y- You are..." Shaking her head she looked at him like she was in a daze.
