Chapter 25

At this time,the bar was packed with people with a little dim light. Mr. Rex and Hilda walked inside.

Hilda's prettyface was a little pale. Her gaze was filled with worry and solemn emotions after all Nathan was no where in sight and Mr. Rex was still clinging onto her.

Thinking about the troubles she was going to face and how her life was going to come to an end, Hilda quivered.

"Hilda why are you acting timid?" Rex complained when he noticed how stiff Hilda was acting. His brows immediately knitted as a thick frown formed on his face.

Hilda then smiled at him and said sweetly. "No, I am not timid.It is just that it is too packed in here."

Rex could not help but start laughing. "This place is always packed like this. This place charges at least ten thousand per person. What a steal for a hillbilly like you. Don't worry.Today,you will get to see various aspects of society."

Hilda took a deep breath as she soothe her angry emotions.Who did he think he his? Just because he his the heir to billions? Smiling at him, she walked towards the inside of the club without uttering a word.

On the first floor was the bar. Once entered, you can hear the

loud roaring of music and people screaming. It was very noisy and dim. Everyone inside was swaying their bodies like wild horses that were let loose after a long time.

With a scene of lively and noisy festivities, people drinking large amount of alcohol and extreme indulgence in bodily pleasures and sexual pleasures, behaviors involving sex, drugs were all taking place in there. Hilda frowned.

Although this is often considered immoral, in a place like Kiki Bees. it was a norm.

Rex was glad when Hilda took the lead to walk inside. In his mind they were going to drink and spend the night in wild rioting and debauchery.

After taking a few steps inside the club, Hilda's eyes were involuntarily attracted to a booth set at the highest point inside the bar.

The booth seat was situated up at the highest part of the bar.Which stood high above everything else looking down from the top as it dominated everything else like a throne where one can only arch their head up to look at.

A thin and dark outline of someone became visible in the dim light as the person sat on the wide booth seat all by himself Jike a king. Taking a closer look, Hilda noticed the person looked familiar.

Sitting up there was a man dressed in a crisp white shirt with black slacks. His looks were of one that would stand out in a crowd but his eyes were cold.

His delicate hands held onto a wine glass as he swayed it around with the air of dignity and disposition, which would make anyone's heart beat uncontrollably.

Rex caught Hilda's gaze when he noticed that she stood still all of a sudden. Walking in front of her with a look of contempt he said. "Never seen it before, have you? Let me tell you. That's the throne of Kiki Bees Club! It is meant only for the owner of the place. Mr. Nathan Clemon. No one else is allowed near the seat."

Nathan Clemon

The name made Hilda's heart jump with joy. Nathan is here.

But at the same time she was nervous. How would he see me when there are so many people in here? I need to get close to him. With that thought in mind Hilda approached Rex looking at him with her big round eyes. "Mr. Rex, How about we join him up there. After all, you are the heir to the Cross family business. You have billions to your name so..."

Rex frowned. I am not sure.

Who hasn't heard of Nathan's ruthlessness. In the big four, He was known for his swift and silent kills that left no bloodstains

and traces. But if there was any blood stains, those were the blood of his dead kills.

Nathan was exceptionally tricky. He engaged in both legal and illegal businesses. No one ever dares to mess with him.

Rex was a little taken back when he heard Hilda's request. But he just shook his head and decided not to be afraid of Nathan as Hilda had a point. He was the heir to billions so he could take this chance and get close to Nathan.

Looking up, he noticed there were men dressed in black guarding the 'throne' seat which meant that Nathan was up there. Immediately, he knew it was his chance since Nathan barely came over there.

Nathan also noticed Hilda when she came in earlier. Looking at her who was coming in, he felt like he had travelled back to fifteen years ago.

At that time, he was just a little man. He followed his dad to the Adams residence on a business invitation. Although he was young at that time, he was already involved in their family business.

After they were done with their business and he was about to head home, he noticed a little girl siting by the garden playing with a doll whiles singing. Her voice was melodious that he was enchanted at that moment. Walking forward he didIn't

notice the girl had her doll's bed lay on the grass and he stepped on it.

The little girl then burst into tears which scared him. Although he was only fifteen at that time, the aura he had with him was way beyond someone of his age.

With a cold face he stepped back and apologized but the girl wouldn't budge till heheld the doll as his dad. From then,anytime he visited the Adams family for business, he was the doll's dad while that little girl was the mother.

A year later he left to continue his studies and never saw that girl again but he never forgot that little girl's charming voice.

After he was all grown up and became the person in charge for the Clemon family and Group, he still had that little girl in mind.

That was until she approached him at the bar.

"Although years have past, you are always going to be that little girl to me. And now I won't let anyone harm you."

Nathan stared at her before he walked down alone.

And this moment Rex held Hilda's hand and ushered her toward the 'throne' seat.

Hilda shivered when Rex held her because she saw Nathan walking towards them with a cold face.

The expression on her face changed completely. As though she was in a frenzy.

When Nathan walked down from the 'throne' seat, the bodyguards wanted to follow along but he stopped them and not many knew him as the Nathan Clemon as he always kept a low profile.

Stopping right in front of Rex and Hilda, Nathan stared at Rex coldly and then his hand which held onto Hilda.

Following his gaze, Hilda pulled her hand out of Rex's grip as she was horrified. But Rex held her firmly that she couldn't pull back her hand.

"What's wrong babe?" Rex asked when he saw Hilda pulling her hands from his grip and the horrified expression on her face.
