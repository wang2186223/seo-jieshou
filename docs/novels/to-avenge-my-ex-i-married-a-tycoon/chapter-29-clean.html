Chapter 29

At the hospital, The members of the Clemon's family took turns in the ward of the Patriarch. Nathan who was currently in the room sighed when he noticed how pale his grandfather was.

The old man was lying on the hospital bed with all kind of tubes and apparatuses inserted into his skinny and frail body.

The mere sight of him lying down like this broke his heart."Grandpa.." Nathan said in a soft whisper.

Looking at the emotionless old man who didn't respond to him once again, Nathan could not help but feel every kind of sadness. Although his facial expression didn't reveal anything,he himself knew how he felt deep within him.

At his age, instead of being in an ICU ward and suffering all by himself, he should be home being surrounded by his kids and grand kids smiling and laughing it off in the back yard garden.

Although it appears to be like he was receiving every possible means of comfort, it was still far different.

Nevertheless, it was a good thing since his dad had noticed him earlier. Otherwise, he would never have guessed about his current situation. Turning around, he walked out.

In the corridor, the Clemons Family members were all gathered there with sorrow written on their faces but deep down within them, they are the only ones who know what is really going on with them.

The old man's sudden collapse had led them all on edge.Others were waiting for his death whiles others want him to open his eyes.

As at now, the old man as given all decision making rights to Nathan which made most of the family members resent him. If he died now, they may take turns and share the property since there won't be any will but if he was to open his eyes, he would make sure to give everything to Nathan before dying.

Although they couldn't fathom why the old man was so fond of Nathan, they knew one thing for sure. Nathan is very capable.

Nathan looked at the members who were standing on the corridor with crocodile tears streaming down their faces and snorted before walking out of the hospital.

Outside the hospital, it was drizzling. While waiting for his car,he stretched out his right hand to catch some rain water until his hand went numb from the cold.

Arriving home, Nathan went into his bedroom. It wasn't until then did he recall that Hilda has now officially moved into his

villa. Looking at her who was lying on the bed clueless about someone standing beside her, Nathan sighed.

Nathan then walked into the bathroom to take a shower. As soon as he was done, he changed into clean clothes and plopped onto the bed ready to fall asleep. But the moment his head touched the pillow, he felt a warm hand on him.

Nathan's eyes darkened. Hilda as sensed him as he plopped beside her. Hilda's temperature was higher than his, and her touch was also burning as if it would make him melt.

Nathan could feel Hilda's breathe just by his neck, and soon,his body started making changes because of the heat.

Nathan recovered from the shock of being unable to move just now and tried to get out of the bed. He didn't want to even think about what would happen if he didn't get out of the bed.

One thing he knew for sure was that, if he let things go on like this,something bad would happen for sure.

"You are entertaining me, huh?" Nathan tried to get up but he also didn't want to wake her up. As he was being cautious, the physical contact with the warmth of her flesh aroused Nathan's desire more and more.

Aware of his increasing excitement, Nathan froze and dared not move again. But he could not help but curse Hilda a thousand times in his heart.

"D*mn it! You're just... you're going to make me take a cold shower at this time!" Nathan whispered to himself.

With that Nathan got off the bed and excited the master's bedroom. Inside one of the guest rooms, Nathan had just had a shower and was thinking about Hilda.

For his past life, no lady had been able to turn him on like Hilda. Anytime he though about her, his body goes through some changes. Thinking about her again, Nathan felt changes in his body again."D*mn it! Not again."

So that night alone, Nathan had to take a cold bath three times in a row.

Early the next morning, Hilda was up early. She looked around and noticed that Nathan was still no where to be found.Sighing she murmured to herself. "He didn't come back last night!"

She then dragged herself into the bathroom to take a shower before heading downstairs.

Downstairs, on the first floor, she could hear the sound of plates clashing. Frowning, she hurried into the kitchen only to see the man cooking again. 'I thought he didn't come back but here he is.' Hilda thought.

"Pumpkin, you are up." Nathan said in a kind of gentle voice.

"Yes, Good morning." Hilda said rather cheerfully.

"Good morning Pumpkin. Hope you slept well?"

"Yes I did. What time did you get back last night?" Hilda asked.

Thinking about last night, Nathan suddenly turned gloomy.After what you did to me last night you are still asking. "When I came back you were already asleep and I didn't want to worry you so I slept in the guest room."

"Oh, okay."

"Come help me with the dishes onto the dinning table." Nathan then handed some of the dishes to Hilda.

At the same time in the hospital, the Patriarch of the Clemons family was still unconscious as he lay motionless on the bed.Nathan's mom and dad were keeping him company.

Mrs. Leah Clemons, Nathan's mom said rather pitifully. "Hunnie, it has been twenty four hours yet dad is still not awake."

As the weak woman she was, Mr. Damian Clemon, Nathan's dad didn't want her to stress herself. "Don't worry Swveetie,Dad would be fine. Let me get you home to rest."

"No Hunnie, I want to be here till dad wakes up." Mrs. Leah Clemons refused.

"But you know your health too. You should not be stressing yourself..." Mr. Damian Clemon didn't finish when the ward door was suddenly opened.

A lady in a sleek dress walked into the ward. She was tall and breathtakingly beautiful with a petite body. She had a shapely figure as her body was kind of imp- thin. She was wasp-waisted and her skin was glossy in complexion. She had slender eyebrows with eyelashes that were velvety. Her ears were sea- nymph with a dainty nose and cute lips.

As she walked in, she smiled and showed her shiny, halo white teeth. With a purse in her hands, her nails were varnished as she placed her purse down. Her hair was ebony black with the edges dyed sunrise gold.

With rapture- blue eyes, she stared at the old man on the hospital bed and pouted her lips and called out in a sweet and soothing voice. "Aunt, uncle"

Mr. and Mrs. Clemon turned around only to see the petite lady.She was Gloria Sanchez. Nathan's betrothed wife when they were kids.

"Gloría, what are you doing here?" Mrs. Leah Clemons was surprise to see her.

"Aunt, I heard about grandpa's illness so I decided to pass by and check up on him and how Nathan is feeling."

"My dear, you are stilI sweet." Gloria was known for her bouncy and joyous personality which was one to behold. Mrs.Leah Clemons was full of smiles as she looked at her.
