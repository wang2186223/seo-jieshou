Chapter 36

Several people were approaching with a woman in the center who was wearing a tailor made dress which accentuated her statuesque body. Her heels contrasted the gleaming marble floor.

'Oh my..' Hilda gasped. Not her again. The lady that arrived at the shop and had caught everyone's attention in a fraction of a second was none other than Carmen, her sister. No step sister.And standing right beside Carmen was none other than Gloria.the lady she had a fight with earlier in front of the restaurant.

As Carmen was a model, she was well none within the entertainment industry. And Gloria was also quite famous among the socialite diva's as she was the president of the Sanchez Entertainment media.

As if sensing someone's gaze on her, Carmen turned to look at her. Hilda's heart skipped a beat that she turned her head back and stared back at the dress.

She shut her eyes and prayed in desperation. 'God please let her not see me! Please let her not see me."

Only then did something hit Hilda. She opened her eyes as she came to the sudden realization that she didn't need to avoid Carmen.

With confidence written on her face, she turned to Naima. "Hey,

do you think I should get this dress?"

However. Naima's attention wasn't on Hilda. Taking Hilda's arm and shaking it. Naima gritted her teeth and said. "It's that b*tch!" She then turned to Hilda and asked with her eyes sparkling with fury. "Do you think I should mess with her?"

Seeing her friends sparkling eyes with fury. Hilda knew she was up to no good. She then rolled her eyes. "Why the hell would you? Don't stoop too low to her level."

Before Naima could say anything, a girl's voice cut in. "I don't think you should buy it. The real question is, can you afford it sis?"

Hilda and Naima both knew who the voice belonged to without having to turn back their heads. It was none other than Carmen. She had her hair dyed dark brown. Breaking away from Gloria, Carmen approached both Hilda and Naima.

She took the dress with a pompous smile on her face and said to the sales lady. "Pack this dress for me."

After which she turned and eyed Hilda from head to toe completely ignoring Naima with a smile of mockery present on her face.

Seems like she is doing really well from when we last met. But she is clearly no match for me as I got Daniel now'. Thinking about that, she smiled brightly that her smile reached all the

way to her ears.

Noticing the smile on Carmen's face blew up Naima's fuse."Why are you looking at her like that? And who said she couldn't afford it?"

With a pompous expression. Carmen glared at Naima sourly."Oh yeah. I know you. What's your name again? Erm...? You are that girl who always follows Hilda around. But sorry.I wasn't talking to you but to my sister."

"Oh? So I can't express my self now?" Taking a few steps forward, she leaned towards Carmen with a sarcastic smile plastered on her face. "Hey, so you know she is your sister but you went after her boyfriend? Just look at yourself. Do you know what people like you are called? A wh*re!"

Carmen's smile froze. She wasn't expecting Naima to say that.She then looked around to make sure no one heard what Naima said. As she was sure no one had heard, she pouted and took Hilda by the hand mustering the most heart breaking voice she could. "Sis, you still think I took Daniel from you?I didn..."

"Enough..." Hilda cut her off and pulled her hand from Carmen's grip. She knew what Carmen wanted to do.

Gloria who was standing were Carmen had left her took a closer look at Hilda and finally recalled her. Her eyes dilated

when she did. 'That is the lady who ruined my bag from earlier.

Before Carmen could say anything, a louad voice was heard which belonged to Gloria.

"If it isn't the lady from earlier." Gloria exclaimed maneuvering her way towards them. "It sure is you!"

Oh. that's the unreasonable woman from earlier. With face full of grit she thought, 'I can't let Carmen and this unreasonable woman spoil my day.'

Carmen was shock before she asked Gloria. "You know her?"

"Of course. She is thed*mnb*tch I told you about from earlier.

The one who ruined my bag." Gloria glared at Hilda as she replied.

"Miss,who are you calling thed*mnb*tch?" Hilda asked through gritted teeth. How dare that unreasonable woman call me a d*mn b*tch?

Naima was lost but Hilda immediately filled her in about all that happened at the entrance of the restaurant earlier that being the reason why she was a little late.

Hearing what Hilda said, Naima glared angrily at Carmen and pointed at her face with her finger. "This is all your doing I know. What else do you want from Hilda after snatching

Daniel away? Eh?"

"What has that got to do with you? She is my sister and I can mess with her whenever I want to. What about you? Who do you think you are? You are nothing but a lap dog who always follows her around like..."

Naima didn't wait for Carmen to finish her sentence. When she heard Carmen calling her a dog she hurriedly interrupted. "Yes I am. What about you? You are nothing but a cheap wh*re who hooks up against her sister's boyfriend at the slighted chance you get."

As Hilda notice that Naima was fuming with anger.she held her and pulled her away. "Don't waste your breath on her.Let's go."

Naima refused to budge. "I have to teach her a lesson." With that said she moved over to Carmen with speed.

Hilda,Gloria. the sales ladies and the onlookers watching were too astonished to move. The seeming small lady Naima suddenly kicked Carmen so hard on the knee making her loose balance. Carmen fell to the ground that it seemed her head would fly off. Then Naima stood over Carmen who gritted her teeth and spat out. "What do you think you are doing? I'm going to kill you! Get off me!"

When Carmen struggled to get back on her feet, Naima

immediately knocked her down again and then siapped fiei a couple of times. "This is for all you have been doing to Hilda."

"She is going to kill her." Gloria exclaimed when she snapped out to reality as her face turn white from watching Naima slap Carmen. "She is going to kill Carmen."

That was when everyone snapped back to their senses and pulled Naima who was still burning with fury from Carmen.

As Carmen was an international model, she was well known and she also has a massive fan base. Some of her fans who were at the scene started yelling at Naima. Other's also wanted to put up a fight with her but Hilda immediately pulled her into the dressing room.

Naima was still fuming with rage when Hilda pulled her into the dressing room. "How dare that b*tch call me a dog? I just want to beat the hell out of her!"

"It's okay Naima. You already thought her a lesson. I am not sure she would ever mess with you again"

Hearing this, Naima calmed down and turned to Hilda with a smirk and raised her eyebrows. "How was I just now?"

Hilda couldn't help but laugh at Naima's facial expression.Giving her a thumps up she said. "You were awesome."

Being complimented, Naima laughed too. "Don't worry. If she

ever messes with you again, I would beat her blue black that no one would recognize her ever again and she wouldn't dare to mess with you again."

"Hahaha...With what you just did, I am very suare she would never want to mess with me again. Let's get going. It is already late." Hilda checked the time.

"Oh, I almost forgot. You are already Mrs. Clemon so you got to cook for our husband. Hahaha.." Naima joked.

Hilda smiled shyly and helped her straighten her disheveled hair and dress before they walked out of the dressing room.
