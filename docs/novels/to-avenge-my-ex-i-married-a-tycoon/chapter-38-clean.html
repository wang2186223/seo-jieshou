Nathan run his fingers gently through her smooth hair. "Let's make this work between us. I think we are a perfect match made in heaven. I'm willing to fulfill my duties as a husband and make you the happiest woman in the whole world."

Hilda was at a loss for words. Seriously? He is so crafty and smooth tongued. He is even sweet talking me. Who would believe this is the cold and aloof CEO Nathan Clemon? Hilda mused.

She opened her mouth to say something and then closed it again. The words were stuck in her throat. Noticing the sight of her opening and closing her mouth with her rosy lips were too much for a man to resist. Nathan had an urge to kiss her again.

He lowered his head and gave her a second kiss.

'That is it.' Hilda pushed him away. "Why do you keep on kissing me?" The touch of his lips on hers and his scent had sent her into a heady trance. He is trying to seduce me.She thought.

"What's wrong with me kissing my wife?" Nathan looked at her in puzzlement.

"Erm.. erm I think.." He did have a point. I am his wife so of course he can kiss me. But I think this is going a little too fast.Although he is trying his best to make it work, we should let it

flow slowly.

Hilda didn't know how to voice out her concerns so she took a few steps back and bumped into Nathan's desk.

Nathan also took a few steps forward. He abruptly pushed her on the desk behind her and leaned forward.

They found their bodies in an awkward yet erotic posture.Hilda could hardly move. She wriggled and tried to get up from the desk but Nathan didn't budge.

"You think?" Nathan raised an eye brow.

Hilda then shook her head.

"Okay, I'm famished now. Let's get going." Nathan then pinched Hilda waist softly.

"Ouch." Hilda uttered a yelp and blushed in embarrassment."

Did we have to talk like this?" No one had ever cornered her in a position like this. Her face was burning hotter.

Nathan smiled and released her and they walked out of the study.

Nathan drove to a high end restaurant with Hilda keeping him company as he ate. After coming out of the restaurant they didn't walk towards their car as Nathan suggested they take a walk.

They walked in a lane hand in hand both keeping their mouth shut as though not to disturb the eerily silent atmosphere around them.

She was thinking, it is so nice being around him. How I would love if it could last longer. Then she caught a glimpse of a cinema not far away. she thought, it would be nice if we could catch a movie too.

Nathan noticed her eyes sparkle with delight when she saw the cinema and decided to take some time to watch a movie. After all they are a couple so they could do what couples do.

For obvious reasons, she then realized that they were heading towards the cinema.

"Nathan." She called.

"Hmm" Nathan responded and turnedd to look at her.

"Are we headed towards the cinema?"

"Yeah. Or? What do you think about catching a movie? I have some time now."

"Sure." Hilda smiled.

Nathan released his hand, took off his suit jacket and draped it over her casual white jacket. The gesture gave her a lot of confidence standing beside him.

While she seemed to fidget, he picked her up and carried her in his arms and then walked on.

Never had a man treated her this way. He treated her like she was his most precious thing in his life.

"Nathan, put me down I can walk." Hilda tried wriggling out of his arms as she felt shy.
