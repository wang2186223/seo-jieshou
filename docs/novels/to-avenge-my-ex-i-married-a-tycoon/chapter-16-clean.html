Chapter 16

"Hilda meet Mr. Rex Cross. He is the Young Master of the Cross Family. Mr. Rex, this is my daughter I told you about."

Looking at Hilda who was simply dressed with a little makeup.Rex's eyes lit up. She is a beauty. Taking her hand Rex spoke up "Hi Miss Hilda, I'm Rex. Nice to meet you."

Frowning Hilda replied. "Hi Mr. Rex, Is a pleasure to meet you.11

With that Mrs. Adams said shamelessly. "I will leave you two to get to know each other. As I said, Mr. Rex, Hilda is single and so are you."

Understanding the meaning of her words, Hilda smiled bitterly and thought. So I'm the bargaining chip here for them to get a marriage alliance with the Cross family. What a shame.

Rex stared internly at Hilda as he thought she isn't so bad after all.

Everyone in Taadi knows who Rex was. He was young but leads a promiscuous life. Looking at him who was staring at her flirticiously, Hilda shuddered.

As they were standing and looking at each other awkwardly,Carmen came over with a drink. "Mr. Rex, Sis, let's have a toast to us."

After taking the drink Hilda didn't think about anything strange and took a mouthful of it.

Carmen started engaging Hilda in a conversion about her and Daniel all which ended awkwardly as Hilda merely snorted at everything she said.

All of a sudden, Hilda noticed that there was some changes in her body as she started feeling hot. "Excuse me, I got to use the washroom." Then Hilda walked away.

Looking at her who was walking towards the washroom,Carmen smiled and followed after her too. "Excuse me Mr.Rex.11

In the bathroom, Hilda splashed water on a face non stop as she felt hot all over. Suddenly Carmen entered. "Sis, are you okay? Looks like you are not alright. Come let me take you to the restroom.

Looking at Carmen who was acting all nice with her Hilda didn't understand but felt panicked. Thinking back, she recalled the wine Carmen handed her. Gritting her teeth she asked."Carmen what did you put in the drink?"

Smiling Carmen replied. "Seems like you are no fool after all.Mr. Rex as already taking a liking to you so..." Carmen stopped mid way and smirked. Taking out her phone she called for two bulky looking men to carry Hilda into the hotel

room.

Trying her best to break free from their grasps Hilda yelled hysterically. "Carmen, You b*tch! I swear I won't let you off the hook if anything happens."

"Haha,you won't let me off. I'm scared sis. What can you do eh? You got no backer now. Daniel isn't with you anymore so you are all alone now." Carmen smirked. At this time they were already inside the hotel room so there was no need for Carmen to pretend to be the caring and kind sister.

Hilda felt hot all over as though she was in a huge oven. It was excruciatingly hot which made her feel overwhelmingly confused.

Looking at her who was in a daze, Carmen went back into the hall. "Mr. Rex, my sister wants to see you."

In the hotel room, Hilda vaguely made out a silhouette.She then felt a cool sensation on her left cheeks before letting out a soft disgruntled murmur. Squinting her eyes, she was taken aback by the figure in front of her.

Feeling weak like jelly, she thought she had just woken up from a dream. She felt like she was walking on the clouds only to fall back down the next second opening her eyes to see a familiar face in front of her.

"Mr. Rex?" She croaked. Hearing her voice she was shocked.

Hilda quickly pushed the man on top of her aside and jumped off the bed. "What did you do to me?"

Rex stroked his chin as his eyes swept up and down her body like a beast hunting his prey. "Are you sure you are unaware or trying to pretend about what's happening?"

Feeling weak. Hilda bumped into a table and fell to the ground.Seeing her state, the man raised his eyebrow slightly and said with a smile. "Your parents said that you are a virgin when they brought you to me."

"What?" Hilda shook her head. The last memory she had was when Carmen brought her into the room. Her words lingered inside her ears. So this is what she meant by Mr. Rex taking a liking to me? Hilda's expression grew more unsightly after seeing the man smirk.

So my parents delivered me to a stranger on my grandmother's 70th birthday amidst the celebration?

Walking close to her, the man bent and lifted her chin only to be hit in return by Hilda. "Get your hands off me! And stay away."

A dangerous glint flashed past the man's eyes at Hilda's words.No longer making any advances at her, he looked on as Hilda continued to struggle. "Tsk, tsk, tsk. What do you think your parents are doing now, along with your beautiful sister and

future brother - in -law?"

At this time. Nathan as already arrived at the venue for the birthday and was looking all over the place for Hilda. Since it was the Matriarch's birthday, he came with the intention of tagging along with a business partner who was close to the Adams family.

As he always kept a low profile, not many people knew him as the CEO of Clemon Group. He dropped down to Taadi with the sole purpose of keeping Hilda company after finding out that she wasn't related to the Adams family.

At the time he spoke to Hilda about the gift he prepared, he was almost done with his meeting and then headed for the airport to board a plane back to Taadi.

After a while of not seeing her, Nathan asked Stanley to hack into the hotel's security system to look for a her.

As the celebration was still ongoing, it was time to cut the cake.

Carmen was holding unto Daniel as she sighed before saying."Daniel, Hilda wasn't feeling well so I took her to one of the rooms prepared for the guests. Let's go and get her before the cake cutting starts."

In the bedroom, Hilda was feeling hot and cold at the same time. The feeling was all too familiar to her. She had once been

given the same drug but the difference was that, the last time it was a stranger but today it was her family. The same people who brought her up for the past few years.

Hilda forcely bit her lower lipsto regain some consciousness."

What are you going to do with me? Let me go and in return if there is anything you need. I'll surely help you out."

The man raised his eyebrows and said with some amusement?"What can you give me? What could I Rex Cross, the young master of the Cross family ever lack?"

Hilda clenched her fists tightly and let her nails dig into her own flesh. The promiscuous Young Master of the cross family.With that her gaze darkened for a second.
