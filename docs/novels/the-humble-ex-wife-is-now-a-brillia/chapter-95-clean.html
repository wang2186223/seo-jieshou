"Fine. I'll agree," Brendon said, his voice gentler now. "Just take care of my grandma."

"I will," Christina replied, her tone frosty, her eyes distant. There was no warmth in her words.

Brendon shifted uncomfortably. The chill in her voice stung, but he still clung to the hope of mending things so that he could establish a connection with someone as powerful as Calvin.
