#Chapter 1

After losing passion with my husband, I asked my best friend to help me relax.

Little did I know she would take me to an unusual club.

That night became an experience I would never forget for the rest of my life.

Yesterday, I had a fierce argument with my husband.

The reason was his inappropriate closeness with a colleague.

He is an executive at an international trade company.

Young, capable, tall, and handsome.

There are many women around him with ulterior motives.

Because of this, I have lost count of how many times we have argued.

Yesterday, on a whim, I brought lunch to his office.

I happened to see a woman with a hot body.

She used the excuse of delivering documents.

Wearing a halterneck dress, she leaned over his shoulder, posing seductively.

My temper flared, and I started arguing with him.

The end result was a unpleasant parting.

That night, my husband did not come home.

He always uses this trick after every argument.

I was shaking with anger but had nowhere to vent.

On a sudden impulse, I went to my best friend's house.

I never expected that this impulsive decision would change the course of my life.

My best friend is Wendy Lowell.

We are very close.

We know each other's door lock passwords.

I called her first, but no one answered.

I thought she was already asleep.

So I went to her house without notifying her.

When I arrived, I used the password to open the security door with ease.

Just as I reached the center of the living room, I heard soft, breathy moans coming from her bedroom.

Hearing the sounds, I was confused.

Isn't Liam on a business trip for a year?

How could he be back so soon?

Then a strange thought suddenly popped into my head.

Wendy is cheating!

I found myself walking to the bedroom door.

Through the slightly open door, I saw two bodies entangled together.

The male body was young and fit.

His broad, strong arms gripped Wendy's slender waist tightly.

Her fair, glowing skin intertwined with his chocolate-colored body.

Accompanied by the sound of fierce collision, I stood there blushing, rooted to the spot.

Wendy really is cheating!

And she actually found a young guy!

Such things, I only dare to fantasize about when I'm lonely.

But beyond the embarrassment, I realized that what I felt more was desire.

After so many years of marriage, I have never experienced a woman's pleasure.

Intimate moments between us were always rushed.

Deep down, I also yearn for a young, strong man to let loose with...

Just as I was engrossed, a door opened from the direction of the bathroom behind me.

Then came a startled voice.

"Who's there?"

My thoughts were quickly pulled back.

The sounds of passion inside the room abruptly stopped.

Wendy and the man inside both turned to look.

...

"I... didn't mean to. I called you, but you didn't answer, so I..."

I was somewhat awkward, stammering an explanation to Wendy.

To my surprise, she waved it off dismissively.

She sent the two young men away from the room.

Then, as if nothing had happened, she pulled me onto the sofa to chat.

"Had another fight with Ethan?"

She looked completely unfazed.

Seeing that she didn't seem to mind what I had just witnessed, I breathed a sigh of relief.

I immediately poured out all my grievances.

To my surprise, after listening, she rolled her eyes at me with disdain.

"If he's fooling around outside, why can't you find someone else?"

Under my shocked gaze, she suddenly started introducing me to the two young men from earlier.

She said they were private fitness trainers.

I've heard of that profession.

It's just using fitness as a pretext for intimate transactions.

So at first, I didn't agree.

Unexpectedly, Wendy, upon hearing this, boldly showed me a video she had taken.

In the video, she lay flat on a soft hotel bed.

Beside her feet, several shirtless muscular men were kissing her feet...

Hearing Wendy's increasingly heavy breathing, I lowered my head in embarrassment.

...

It would be a lie to say I wasn't tempted.

But after all, I am an educated adult.

Such morally questionable actions made it hard for me to convince myself.

"No... I'm a married woman. If Ethan finds out..."

"You care so much about Ethan, but does he ever consider you?"

Wendy impatiently cut me off.

Then she grabbed my hand.

"Chloe, I can't stand your hesitation. Come out with me right now!"

That night, I half-heartedly put on the clothes Wendy prepared for me.

Then she took me to a KTV in the city center...
