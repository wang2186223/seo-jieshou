Chapter 49

The next day, as the sun shone through the window into the ward, the fast asleep Hilda turned and tried to face away from the sun rays ready to fall back asleep when she was suddenly jolted awake by a voice. "Want to get back to sleep again?"

She then opened her eyes only to find herself in the hospital ward. She was having a nice dream and had long forgotten to wake up only to be woken up by Nathan. She looked around and spotted Nathan in the sofa going through some files.

She then yawned. "Yeah. Good morning."

"Good morning. You should hurry and freshen up.We will be leaving after breakfast. You are being discharged"

Hilda who hated being in the hospital was happy that they were about to leave but mad at the fact that Nathan had woken her up from her dream.

She sulked and got down from the bed before entering into the bathroom to brush her teeth and freshen up. After she was done,they had lunch and Nathan packed up the things he had brought for her that night into a little bag and then they walked out of the ward together.

When they got into the car, Nathan thought as if he had just remembered something and announced. "There would be a birthday party tomorrow night and I want you to come with

me."

"A birthday party? Whose birthday party? And are you sure you want me to come along with you? Are you not afraid of what people would think when they see us together?" Hilda bombarded Nathan with questions after he asked her to tag along with him to the birthday party.

To her, tagging along with Nathan to a birthday party is like them announcing their relationship to the outside world.

Nathan was amused by the series of questions thrown at him by Hilda. Acting laconic. Nathan turned to her and gave his phone with a picture on it to her. "Don't worry about what others might think. We would just keep a close distance."

Hilda who had Nathan's phone in her hand looked at the picture with a short bio and invitation of the birthday celebrant and to her surprise it was Christopher Anderson. She was beyond thrilled as she looked at hispicture.

He was her favorite actor. Although she wasn't a crazy fan of his, she was a fan who wouldn't let the chance of meeting her celebrity crush slip away.

Nathan who was in the driver's seat stole glances at Hilda who was sitting beside him and his expression turned grim. Hilda was still admiring Christopher's picture.

When they got back home, Hilda received a call from Naima.

They chatted cheerfully on the phone that she ignored Nathan and walked into the hall laughing and giggling.She told Naima about how Nathan came to save her at the Adams villa.

"And then? You gave yourself to him to thank him right?"Naima was so excited that she couldn't wait for Hilda's reply.

After a pause. Hilda answered rather bashed. "No."

The unrommantic answer left Naima in worry. After a long pause she finally calmed herself down and asked. "Babes, don't tell me you guys haven't banged?"

"Bang?" Hearing the brazenness of Naima left goose bumps on HiIda as she recalled that night.

"Come on Hilda, don't tell me you don't know what it means to get banged." She rolled her eyes and continued. "Use google to search for the meaning if you don't get it?"

"It's not like I don't understand what you meant though, it is the salacious way you put it that made me cringe.Jeez,you make me flinch." Hilda replied after feeling uneasy hearing how straight forward Naima said the word.

"Take it easy babes." Naima giggled as she replied.

Hilda and Naima had known each other for a long time. They have been best friends for a long time. Although they both travelled miles apart, they didn't forget to stay in touch. Naima

then urged on. "You know countless women in this world would die to be Nathan's woman. Count me in. I wouldn't mind being his second wife if he practice polygamy. Right now.you have the best chance to win his heart otherwise you would lose him."

She then went on. "I know you are a smart girl. I believe you would hack what it takes to make him yours alone."

"But I didn't say we haven't done it yet..." Hilda replied shyly.

"You are such an idiot! Why didn't you tell me earlier."

"Fuck off! Why is there the need?" Hilda shouted laughing.

To which Naima asked. "Hilda, I'm really curious. Who is on top and who is under. You or who?"

Hilda who was blushing at the moment as she thought about what happened the other night didn't know how to reply.She pretended not to hear her question and changed the topic.They spoke till late in the afternoon when Nathan who had busied himself in the study came to get her so they go out for lunch.

In the Clemons family hospital, Mrs. Clemons had an invitation card in her hand which she intends to give to Gloria so she could have some time with Nathan the day after.

It was the invitation to Christopher's birthday party. Although she was not that well when it came to her health, Christopher didn't forget to give her an invitation.

And the fact she couldn't go, she needed someone to go in her stead so she figured Gloria would be the perfect person as she had always seen her as a daughter in law.

Now in the ward, she gave the invitation card to her. "Gloria.tomorrow night is Christopher's birthday dinner. As you know,your aunt isn't that well so she won't be able to go. I would be counting on you to go in my stead."

Gloria became thrilled immediately she heard that it was an invitation to Christopher's birthday party. She knew for sure that Nathan would be there. As Nathan and Christopher had been friends for a long time now, there was no way that Nathan wouldn't attend the birthday party.

Since she came back to X country, she had been looking for ways to get close to Nathan but she hadn't found any yet. Any time she tries to meet Nathan, he would either avoid her or give her the cold shoulder. Now this would be the chance for her to spend time with Nathan.

Thinking about this, she tookthe invitation card and sighed."I would love to go aunt. But you know, the venue is onthe island between X country and Y country. There is no way I would be able to get there without a date."

Hearing this. Mrs. Clemon thought what she said made sense."Don't worry about that. Nathan would be going too so I would be sure to ask him to go with you and keep you company."

When Gloria heard this, she was beyond thrilled but asked unenthusiastically. "Aunt, I wouldn't want it to be like I might be a third wheel. What if Nathan already have a date?"

Mrs. Clemons smiled. "Nathan, a date? I am not sure. It might be him going with one of his secretaries and nothing else."

"Really? So aunt I would be going with Nathan?"

The Patriarch also thought that would be a good chance for them to rekindle their feelings so he replied even before Mrs.Clemons could. "Of Course. I would call him and inform him now."

With that the patriarch took out his phone and gave Nathan a call."Nate.."

Nathan who was sitting in the living room with Hilda watching a comedy show that Hilda loved suddenly stood up after receiving his grandpa's call. "Grandpa.."

"What time would you be leaving for Christopher's birthday party tomorrow?" The Patriarch who didn't like beating around the bush when talking cut straight to the point immediately Nathan answered the called.

Nathan was confused by his grand father's question but then he answered. "Tomorrow around noon."

"Okay. I would send someone to accompany you. Be sure to take care of her. She is my guest."

"Grandpa who?" Nathan had a fair idea but wanted to be sure.

But his grandfather didn't bother answering. "Be sure to be nice. Tomorrow then." With that he hanged up.
